How it started?

I was interested in photography since I was very young and could only afford to buy a camera after graduating. My fist camera was a Fuji bridge camera which had all the settings to play with. learned a lot of things in this camera. Got my first DSLR Canon 1100d after using fuji for a year. With 18-55 kit lens got a 55-250 Zoom lens. 55-250 Zoom lens gave amazing results. With 250mm lens started clicking birds, insects and nature around me. This is how I developed interest in birds initially. After exploring a few cameras’ I am currently settled with Canon 80D and Tamron 150-600 lens.

Birding

Then I met my buddy Mr. Derrel Pinto almost after 10 years. We were buddies since Pre university days and birding has brought us together once again.

Hope you are in a better place sir and will always be missed.
